About Health Horizon

Health Horizon has been a pillar of the Riverview City community for nearly two decades, with a steadfast commitment to delivering high-quality healthcare. Founded with the vision of transforming local healthcare access, we started as a small family clinic in 2005, built on the principles of integrity, trust, and service. Today, Health Horizon has evolved into a comprehensive medical institution that treats thousands of patients each year. Our commitment to compassionate, expert care remains the core of who we are, ensuring that every patient who walks through our doors feels respected, valued, and heard. We understand that seeking medical care can be challenging, so we strive to create an environment that fosters trust, confidence, and support for patients and their families.

Our Mission and Vision

Our mission at Health Horizon is to bridge the gap between medical expertise and compassionate care. We believe that every person deserves quality healthcare that addresses not only their physical symptoms but also their emotional well-being. With a focus on patient empowerment, we encourage individuals to take an active role in their healthcare decisions, providing them with the education and resources needed to make informed choices. Our vision is a healthier, more resilient community where preventive care, early diagnosis, and holistic wellness are accessible to all. By expanding our facilities, embracing the latest in medical technology, and continuously improving our services, we are dedicated to making this vision a reality for our community.

Our Location and Facilities

Situated at street 2, Jaipur, Rajasthan, Health Horizon was designed with convenience and comfort in mind. Our modern facility is not just a place of treatment; it’s a sanctuary of healing and support. Our easily accessible location, just minutes from parking facilities, allows patients and visitors to reach us without hassle. Inside, Health Horizon boasts cutting-edge operating theaters, a dedicated maternity wing, outpatient consultation rooms, and extensive diagnostic labs, each tailored to meet the highest standards in medical care. We’ve also dedicated entire floors to specialized care units, including intensive care, rehabilitation, and oncology, ensuring our patients receive comprehensive treatment under one roof. To foster a welcoming atmosphere, we’ve created relaxing spaces throughout, including a family-friendly waiting area, a prayer room, and an outdoor courtyard that allows patients and visitors to take a moment of peace amidst nature.

Our History and Growth

Health Horizon’s journey began humbly, with a small but dedicated team of doctors and nurses focused on providing family medicine and emergency care. Guided by a passion for helping others, we expanded over the years, adding specialized departments to meet the needs of our growing community. Today, we operate as a comprehensive, multi-specialty hospital, with over 300 healthcare professionals across 15 medical specialties. Our Cardiology, Pediatrics, Neurology, and Orthopedics departments have been recognized for their excellence, and our facilities now include some of the most advanced imaging and diagnostic technology available. Each phase of our growth has been patient-focused, with improvements and expansions implemented to ensure the highest quality of care. We’re proud to serve as a top-tier healthcare provider and trusted resource for the Riverview City community.

Why Choose Health Horizon?

Health Horizon stands out as a hospital that combines cutting-edge medicine with a genuine commitment to patient well-being. We believe that every patient deserves a personalized approach to their healthcare, and our team of doctors, specialists, and support staff work together to provide treatment plans tailored to each individual’s needs.
