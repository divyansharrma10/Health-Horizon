Cardiology Department

Our Cardiology Department is dedicated to the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of heart-related conditions. With advanced diagnostic equipment and a skilled team of cardiologists, we provide comprehensive cardiac care, including management of heart disease, hypertension, arrhythmias, and more.

Orthopedics Department

The Orthopedics Department at Health Horizon focuses on treating musculoskeletal conditions, from fractures and joint issues to sports injuries and arthritis. Our team of orthopedic surgeons and physiotherapists work together to provide both surgical and non-surgical options, helping patients regain mobility and improve their quality of life.

Pediatrics Department

Our Pediatrics Department is dedicated to providing specialized healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents. Our pediatricians focus on a wide range of health services, including routine check-ups, vaccinations, and treatment of childhood illnesses.

Neurology Department

The Neurology Department specializes in diagnosing and treating disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spine, and nerves. Our neurologists handle a variety of conditions, such as epilepsy, stroke, multiple sclerosis, and neurodegenerative diseases.
