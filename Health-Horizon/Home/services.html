our services

Easy Appointment Scheduling

Booking an appointment at Health Horizon has never been easier. We offer a simple, user-friendly online booking system that allows you to schedule consultations with just a few clicks. You can choose from available time slots that suit your schedule and select your preferred healthcare provider. For those who prefer to book over the phone, our dedicated support team is available to assist with appointment scheduling from 8 AM to 8 PM, Monday through Saturday. We understand that healthcare needs can arise unexpectedly, so we also offer same-day appointments for urgent cases.

Online Consultations

Our online consultation service provides expert medical care from the comfort of your home. Through our secure telemedicine platform, you can connect with our doctors and specialists for virtual check-ups, follow-ups, and general consultations. This service is especially beneficial for those with mobility limitations, busy schedules, or those living in remote areas. With features like video chat, secure messaging, and file sharing for reports, our online consultations offer the same level of quality and attentiveness as in-person visits.

In-Person Consultations

For patients who prefer or require face-to-face interactions, Health Horizon provides personalized in-person consultations across all our departments. Our doctors take the time to understand each patient’s individual concerns, conducting thorough examinations and tailoring treatment plans accordingly. Whether you’re here for a routine physical, specialist consultation, or second opinion, our team is dedicated to making your experience comfortable and informative. Patients can access a wide range of in-person services, from physical exams to diagnostic tests and specialist referrals, all in one location.

Flexible Payment Options

At Health Horizon, we strive to make healthcare accessible to everyone by offering flexible payment solutions. Our billing department provides multiple payment options, including credit and debit card payments, online banking, and cash payments at the hospital. For those needing financial support, we also offer payment plans that allow you to spread out costs over time. Additionally, we accept major insurance providers, and our team is available to assist with insurance claims, pre-authorization, and any questions you may have about coverage.

Emergency Care

Health Horizon’s emergency care services are available 24/7, with a dedicated team of doctors, nurses, and support staff ready to handle urgent medical situations. Our emergency department is fully equipped to manage a range of emergencies, including trauma, cardiac events, respiratory issues, and more. We prioritize patient safety and comfort in all emergency cases, ensuring that patients receive prompt attention and stabilization upon arrival. Our emergency team coordinates closely with specialists across our departments, allowing for immediate follow-up care and comprehensive treatment.

Pharmacy Services

Our on-site pharmacy offers convenience and efficiency, providing easy access to prescribed medications and health products. With our knowledgeable pharmacists on hand, patients can have their prescriptions filled immediately following a consultation, saving time and ensuring continuity of care. We stock a wide range of medications, including specialty prescriptions, and work with our patients to help them understand their medications and manage their health more effectively.

Diagnostic and Imaging Services

Our hospital is equipped with a full range of advanced diagnostic tools and imaging services, enabling us to offer precise and timely diagnoses. From MRI and CT scans to X-rays, ultrasound, and lab testing, Health Horizon provides all essential diagnostic services in-house. Each test is performed by skilled technicians and analyzed by experienced specialists, ensuring accurate results and timely reporting. We work to accommodate patients’ schedules by offering flexible appointments for diagnostic tests, so you can get the answers you need without unnecessary delays.

Specialized Departments

Health Horizon is home to multiple specialized departments, including Cardiology, Orthopedics, Pediatrics, Neurology, and more. Each department is staffed by experts in their field, equipped with advanced technology, and committed to delivering high-quality, compassionate care. Our specialists work collaboratively across departments to address complex cases, ensuring that each patient receives comprehensive and coordinated treatment.
